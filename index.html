Grilled chicken sandwich

Ingredients

Chicken breast: 2 breasts - Italian bread: a piece (ciabatta bread) - Pesto sauce: as needed - Tomato sauce: 2 tablespoons - Oil: 2 tablespoons - Salt: a pinch - Black pepper: a pinch - Cheddar cheese: 2 slices - Lettuce: 4 leaves (Italian) - Tomato: 1 piece (cut into slices) - Lemon juice: a quarter cup

How to prepare

1- Mix the pesto sauce and lemon juice in a bowl. 2-Sprinkle pepper and salt on the chicken. 3- Dip the chicken in the pesto sauce well. 4- Cook the chicken pieces in a tray in the oven until cooked. 5- Spread the tomato sauce on the bottom layer of the ciabatta bread, then place the lettuce on top. 6- Add the hot chicken, then the cheese and tomatoes, and serve it.

Crispy fried chicken fingers with cheese

Ingredients

Chicken breast: three-quarters of a kilogram (fingers clipped) Eggs: 2 tablets Bread crumbs: a cup Parmesan cheese: a cup Olive oil: 2 tablespoons Butter: 2 tablespoons Salt: a pinch Black pepper: a pinch Vegetable oil: as needed

How to prepare:

1- Preheat the oven to 180 degrees Celsius. 2- Grease the chicken fingers with a little oil and fry them in the oven, then take them out and leave them aside until they cool down. 3- In a bowl, whisk the eggs with the olive oil. 4- In another bowl, mix the bread crumbs with the Parmesan cheese. 5- Put the fried chicken fingers in salt and black pepper. 6- Dip the chicken fingers in the egg mixture, then in the cheese mixture until they are completely coated. 7- Heat the vegetable oil in a frying pan over medium heat, then add the butter until it melts in the oil. 8- Fry the chicken fingers until they turn golden, then place them on paper towels to absorb the oil, place them on a serving plate, and serve them hot.

Fried chicken breast burger

Ingredients

Chicken Marinade Ingredients: Chicken breast: 3 breasts (cut in half) Yogurt: 2 cups Hot pepper: half a teaspoon Garlic powder: 2 tsp Salt: one teaspoon Black pepper: a pinch

- Burger buns: 6 pieces Lettuce: 3 cups Mayonnaise: 3 tablespoons Tomatoes: 2 pieces (cut into slices) Onion: 1 piece (sliced) Cheese: 6 slices Flour: 2 cups

How to prepare

1- In a bowl, put the chicken breasts, yogurt, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper, and mix the ingredients well. 2- Cover the bowl with cling film and place it in the refrigerator for 4 hours. 3- Dip the chicken pieces in the flour mixture. 4- In a saucepan, heat plenty of oil and fry the chicken pieces. 5- Grease the bottom of the bread with mayonnaise, put cheese, a piece of fried chicken, tomatoes, lettuce, and onions on it, and cover the ingredients with the top of the bread, then serve it.

Turkey sandwich with melted cheddar cheese

Ingredients

Cheddar cheese: half a cup (grated) Toast: 4 slices (preferably thick) Mayonnaise: 2 tablespoons Turkey breast: 2 slices

How to prepare

1- Spread mayonnaise on the slices of bread. 2- Spread the cheddar cheese over the mayonnaise, then put the turkey slices on top of the cheese. 3- Close the slices of bread on top of each other and place them in the pan for about two minutes on each side, until the cheese melts and browns. 4-Put the sandwich on a serving plate and serve it.

Fryer pizza

Ingredients

Flour: a cup Baking powder: one teaspoon Tomato sauce: a cup (for pizza) Mushroom: a cup - Bell pepper: 1 piece (chopped) Yogurt: a quarter cup Mozzarella: a cup (grated) Vegetable oil: one tablespoon - Salt, to taste Thyme: 1 teaspoon (crushed) Black olives: half a cup

How to prepare

1- In a mixing bowl, put the flour, baking powder, yogurt, and salt, and mix the mixture until a cohesive dough is obtained. Cover the bowl, and leave it aside until the dough rests. 2- Heat a non-stick frying pan over a low heat, add the dough and spread it in the pan, then make holes in it with a fork. 3- Grease the dough with the sauce, and add mushrooms, olives, and pepper. 4- Add the cheese, cover the pan, and leave it on the stove until the cheese melts and the dough is cooked, and served hot.

Cheese steak sandwich

Ingredients

- Beef fillet: 500 grams (cut into longitudinal slices) Onion: 1 piece (chopped into thin slices) - Green pepper: 1 piece (chopped slices) Butter: a tablespoon Olive oil: one tablespoon Salt: one teaspoon Black pepper: a quarter of a teaspoon Cheddar cheese: 4 slices - French bread: loaf (large size / cut in half)

How to prepare

1- Heat the butter and oil in a large frying pan. 2- Add the meat, season with salt and pepper, and stir-fry the meat over high heat for 3 minutes, until brown. 3- Reduce the heat, add the onions and green peppers, and stir until the meat is tender and the onions and peppers are tender. 4- Spread the mixture in the French bread and spread the cheese slices over it. 5- Heat the sandwich on the grill until it is roasted and the cheese melts, and serve it hot.

Crispy fried potatoes

Ingredients

Potatoes: 3 grains (peeled and sliced) White vinegar: half a cup Starch: 2 tbsp Oil: abundant (for frying) - Salt, to taste

How to prepare

1- In a bowl, put the potato slices and cover them with water and white vinegar, stir, and leave them aside for 20 minutes, then drain them from the water. 2-Put the potatoes on a piece of cloth and dry them well with paper tissues. 3- In another bowl, put the potatoes and starch, and stir well until the ingredients interfere. 4- Heat the oil, fry the potatoes until they are tender, and serve them hot with a pinch of salt.

Salami and mozzarella pizza in the pan

Ingredients for the dough: Flour: a cup Warm water: a third of a cup Salt: a pinch Sugar: one teaspoon Yeast: a teaspoon Baking powder: a quarter of a teaspoon

Filling ingredients: Pizza sauce: as needed Mozzarella cheese: as needed Salami: slices (to taste)

How to prepare

1- Put the flour, salt, sugar, baking powder and yeast in a deep bowl. 2- Add water to the flour mixture, then start kneading the ingredients until you have a cohesive dough. 3- Roll out the dough in a circular motion, then place it in a pan greased with a little oil. 4- Spread the pizza sauce on the surface of the dough, then add a layer of mozzarella cheese. 5- Place the salami slices evenly on top. 6- Cover the pan with a tight-fitting lid, then leave for 10 minutes, until tender.
